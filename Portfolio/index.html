about me

Hi, my name is Kevand and i'am young music producer from Poland. My carrer started in early 2017 with the joke remix of Satisfaction by Benny Benassi. The important note about me, is that i make music for myself. I do not make music for mainstream, or for specific fanbase. My music, is music that i like. Mostly i make EDM, but maybe in the future u will see me in other genre. For me, anonymity is important. I do not want to be recognizable on the street or anywhere else and I do not want my identity to be seen.
